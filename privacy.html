Privacy Policy

Anchor · Effective May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Anchor (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Anchor mobile application (the “App”) and related services. Anchor is an aquarium controller app that lets you monitor and manage compatible Anchor hardware devices.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. Account authentication is handled by Google Firebase Authentication.

Device and aquarium data. When you connect Anchor hardware or configure tanks in the App, we store data such as device settings, schedules, sensor readings, tank parameters, firmware versions, and system status. This data is associated with your account and stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database.

Push notification tokens. If you allow notifications, we collect and store a device push token (via Expo and Apple Push Notification service) so we can send alerts about your aquarium and devices.

Local app preferences. The App may store preferences on your device (such as your timezone and login session identifiers) using local device storage.

AI assistant usage. If you use the in-app AI assistant, the text and optional images you submit are sent to our backend and processed by Google Gemini to generate responses. We do not use this content for advertising or sell it to third parties.

Permissions-based data. The App may request access to:

  • Local network — to discover and communicate with Anchor devices on your Wi‑Fi network.
  • Location (while in use) — required by iOS to scan for Wi‑Fi networks when pairing devices. We do not use location for tracking.
  • Camera and photo library — only when you choose to take or select a photo within the App.
  • Notifications — to deliver alerts you opt into.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Sync your aquarium and device settings across sessions
  • Send push notifications about device status and alerts
  • Provide AI-powered aquarium guidance within the App
  • Maintain, secure, and improve the App and our services
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers to operate the App, including:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and cloud database
  • Google Gemini — AI assistant responses
  • Expo / Apple Push Notification service — push notifications on iOS

These providers process data on our behalf according to their own privacy policies and our instructions. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers that help us operate the App (listed above)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights, users, or safety
  • With your consent

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and aquarium data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and associated cloud data at any time from Settings in the App. Local data on your device may remain until you uninstall the App or clear app storage.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections and Firebase security rules, to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal information. You can:

  • Update your password in the App
  • Delete your account and data from Settings
  • Disable notifications in your device settings
  • Revoke camera, location, or network permissions in your device settings

To exercise other privacy rights, contact us at engineering@full-stack-engineering.com.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. International Users

Your information may be processed and stored in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

10. Tracking

Anchor does not use your data for cross-app tracking or advertising. We do not request App Tracking Transparency permission for advertising purposes.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page and update the effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
